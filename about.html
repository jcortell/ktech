Mission

Tag line:

About us

Kanteron is a privately held healthcare Computational Bioinformatics

company that provides healthcare providers manage imaging and genomic

data effectively for precision medicine applications at the point of

care.

With presence in Europe and Americas, the Kanteron solution has been tested and validated at some of the worlds largest healthcare networks like the NHS in UK and IMSS in Mexico. Its new version is being developed and tested in Asia before global launch.
