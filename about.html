About us

kanteron.tech has been formed by an experienced team of investors and technologists to help healthcare providers manage radiology and pathology imaging data effectively for precision medicine applications at the point of care.


We'll do that by leveraging Kanteron Systems' technology, which was deployed over 500 times in 15 countries, at some of the world's largest healthcare networks like the NHS in the UK, ISSSTE and IMSS in Mexico, or EsSalud in Peru. Our objective is to bring this technology to the rest of the world!
