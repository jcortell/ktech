QUALITY REGISTRATIONS ACHIEVED BY KANTERON SYSTEMS

CE

European CE Mark and Export Certificate Annex 1 and 2 number 1080/2020-CLV issued by the Spanish Agency for Medications and Healthcare Products (AEMPS), and Spanish Medical Device Manufacturer License number 7506-PS, as required by Article 12 of the Medical Devices Directive 93/42/EEC and conformity assessment for

medical devices in section 2.5 of Guidance MEDDEVs.

FDA

FDA regulatory status (as medical digital data system, medical image storage device, and medical image communications device) according to the 21st Century Cures Act (Cures Act Public Law No. 114-255) Sect. 3060/C/D/E excluded from definition of device software.

ISO 9001 and 13485

Quality system EN ISO 9001:2008 certified by Bureau Veritas (BVQi) number ES112294-1 (04/C-SC004 ENAC), and ISO 13458 number ES111851-1.

Institutions

Institutions from Regional (such as The Chamber of Commerce or the Valencia Exports Institute IVEX), National (such as The Spanish International Trade Institute ICEX or the National Innovation Corporation ENISA), and EU levels (through the European Fund for Regional Development and the Gateway Program) recognized Kanteron Systems relevance, by approving us in several R&D and collaboration programs (such as PIPE, the Corp. Innovation Promotion Plan, or APEX).

Memberships

Kanteron Systems was a member/participant/supporter of the AI in Health Partner Alliance, Horos Project, OpenCB, dcm4che, OHIF, VIT Salud, the Global Alliance for Genomics and Health, Genomic Medicine Alliance (GA4GH), BioVal, ITI, and IHE International.

Compliance

Kanteron Systems complied with the Universal Guidelines for AI, Electronic Industry Citizenship Coalition Code of Conduct (EICC Code of Conduct) and the products and services were covered by Product Liability Insurance provided by Chubb Insurance Company. Kanteron Systems earned the Cyber Essentials badge from the UK Government for following computer security best practices.
